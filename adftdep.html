Department of AD & FT

The professional diploma course in Apparel Designing & Fabrication Technology was started at our polytechnic in the year 2003-04 with the intake of 30 students. We are proud for the fact that the stream of diploma in AD&FT course was introduced at our institution for first time in entire district of Tumkur. So, we are pioneer in starting this course at Tumkur. This Diploma course equip students with an extensive theoretical knowledge as well as it Develops professional & practical skills. The course is delivered by using modern means of educational techniques & computerized designing skills which are an integral part of the curriculum & the modern society. This course provides a solid foundation to build successful career in the booming field of Fashion designing. Students are exposed to industrial visit at textile Units, Garment Factories & Boutiques during their course of studies. After completion of this course students can join 2nd year B.Sc (FAD) through lateral entry. After B.Sc they can also join M.Sc in Textiles & Clothing Apparel Technology Management and M.Sc Fashion Designing. Apparel Designing & Fabrication Technology is a course that expects no extra ordinary skills but it promises glorious future.
