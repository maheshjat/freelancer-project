Vision & Mission

We realize that Education is fundamental for the complete development of individual. As a technical institute, we endeavor to harness this inherent potential through meeting the growing needs of Technical Education. As we grow, we will adapt newer technologies, methodologies and resources and to ensure this we will attract the faculty of the highest order who will compliment our excellent facilities.To achieve this, Our Institution will ensure the Highest Quality of Faculty, Resources and Infrastructure.

These will be structured with a focus Academic Excellence and vercity in approach that will meet the ever increasing needs of students.Supporting this is the Polytechnic's commitment to Education and Research. This will encourage and Assure students in their pursuit of knowledge. All this is a reflection of the Institute's Responsibility to build a Society, that is vibrant and growing through knowledge sharing and enhancing.
