Department of Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering branch is one of the oldest and multi skill oriented branch in the field of engineering as well as science & technology. It provides excellent learning and research opportunitius for diploma students. The study of mechanical engineering may be a basic for future technical studies. this course gives a perception on which to build complex technical knowledge and offers a comprhenceve survey of the fundamental of manufacturing process. There is hardly any aspect of life that is not influenced by the mechanical engineering. As a student in the field of mechanical engineering, they will research, develop, design, namufacture and test tools, engines, machines and other mechanical devices.They work on power generators ,internal combustion engines, steam & gas turbines and jet & rocket engins, can also develop machines such as refrigertor and air conditioners,robots,meterials handling system and industrial production equipments.
