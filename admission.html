Eligibility for Karnataka Students

  • A student must pass in Higher Secondary Board Examination conducted by the Government of Karnataka
  • A student must pass in I.T.I for lateral entry (Direct 2nd year diploma).
  • Minimum age is 16 years to join Diploma in Automobile Engineering.
